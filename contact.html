What types of projects are you interested in?

I'm particularly passionate about web development projects, educational tools, SaaS applications, and solutions that solve real-world problems. I enjoy working across the stack, from frontend interfaces to backend systems. Current interests include:

  • Responsive web applications
  • Educational technology platforms
  • Automation tools and scripts
  • Data visualization dashboards
  • Progressive Web Apps (PWAs)

Are you available for freelance work or internships?

Yes! I'm actively seeking both freelance opportunities and internship positions. As a Computer Science student, I have flexible hours and can dedicate substantial time to meaningful projects. Here's what I offer:

Freelance: Web development, debugging, small to medium projects
Internships: 20-40 hours per week, remote or local in Bicol Region
Collaboration: Open to team projects and startup ideas

Do you offer mentorship or help for beginners?

Absolutely! As someone who's still learning and growing in tech, I understand the importance of mentorship. I'm happy to share what I've learned with fellow students and beginners. Here's how I can help:

  • Code reviews and best practices guidance
  • Career advice for Computer Science students
  • Resources for learning web development
  • Portfolio and project feedback
  • Interview preparation tips

Feel free to ask questions about web development, computer science concepts, or getting started in tech. I believe in paying forward the help I've received.
