Early 2024

Before entering college, I faced the universal question: "What path should I take?" I sought a career that offered both financial stability and personal fulfillment. After extensive research and self-reflection, I discovered Computer Science - a field that perfectly aligned with my analytical mindset and creative problem-solving abilities.
